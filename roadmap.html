Frequently Asked Questions

Are the roadmaps free to access?

Yes! All roadmaps are completely free for students to explore and use.

Is there a way to request a new roadmap if my career choice isn’t listed?

Yes, you can fill out a request form or contact us directly through email/socials. We prioritize roadmaps based on community demand.

How do I know which roadmap is right for me?

Each roadmap includes a short description, required skills, and career outcomes so you can choose the one that best fits your goals.

Is there a way to request a new roadmap if my career choice isn’t listed?

Yes, you can fill out a request form or contact us directly through email/socials. We prioritize roadmaps based on community demand.

Can volunteers contribute to creating or improving roadmaps?

Yes, as a student-led organization we welcome volunteers to join and contribute.

Where can I get notified about new roadmap releases?
